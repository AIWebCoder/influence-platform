## 🇬🇧 ENGLISH — How to Use the Influence Platform

### 🐴 Welcome, Little Donkey! Let's Learn Together! 🐴

Hey there! If you're reading this, it means you want to understand how this cool app works. Don't worry - I'll explain everything in simple words, like I'm talking to a friendly donkey who just woke up! 🐴✨

---

### 📱 What is This App? (Qu'est-ce que cette application?)

Imagine you have a magic robot that can:
- **Create posts** for your Instagram automatically (like a robot writer!)
- **Publish them** at the perfect time (like a robot postal worker!)
- **Watch your accounts** to make sure they're healthy (like a robot doctor!)
- **Test different versions** to see what works best (like a scientist donkey!)

That's exactly what the **Influence Platform** does! It's like having 100 little robot helpers working for you 24/7.

---

### 🚪 How to Enter (Comment entrer)

1. Open your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari - whatever you like!)
2. Type: **http://localhost:3000**
3. You'll see a login page
4. Enter your username and password
5. Click "Sign In" and boom! You're in! 🎉

---

Here's what each button does:

The Dashboard is like the cockpit of a spaceship! It's the first thing you see when you log in.

- **Generation Queue** - How many posts are waiting to be created

**What's on the page:**
- 📈 **Performance Chart** - A graph showing how your accounts are doing over time
- ⭐ **Top Content** - Your best-performing posts
- 💡 **Recommendations** - Smart suggestions from the AI

**What you can do:**
- Just look at it to get an overview!
- The numbers update automatically

### 👥 PAGE 2: ACCOUNTS (COMPTES)

**What you'll see:**
A list of all your social media accounts (Instagram, Twitter, etc.)

**The table shows:**
| Column   |                     What It Means                     |
|----------|-------------------------------------------------------|
| Username | The account name (like @mycoolaccount)                |
| Platform | Which social media (Instagram, Twitter, etc.)         |
| Status   | Is it active or not?                                  |
| Health   | How healthy the account is (green = good, red = bad!) |

**Buttons you'll see:**
- ➕ **Add Account** - Add a new social media account
- ✏️ **Edit** - Change account details
- 🗑️ **Delete** - Remove an account

**How to add a new account:**
1. Click the ➕ button
2. Fill in the username
3. Choose the platform (Instagram, etc.)
4. Add your login credentials
5. Click Save and... DONE! 🎉

### 🛡️ PAGE 3: ACCOUNT HEALTH (SANTÉ DES COMPTES)

**What you'll see:**
A health dashboard for all your accounts - like a doctor's office for your social media!

**What the colors mean:**
- 🟢 **Green (80-100%)** - Super healthy! Your account is happy!
- 🟡 **Yellow (50-79%)** - Needs attention, but okay
- 🔴 **Red (0-49%)** - In danger! Might get banned!

**Health checks include:**
- 📛 Ban detection - Is Instagram angry at you?
- 🌑 Shadowban detection - Is your reach limited secretly?
- 📊 Engagement metrics - Are people interacting with you?
- ⏰ Warmup status - Is your account "warming up" safely?

**What you can do:**
- Click on an account to see detailed health info
- See recommendations to improve health

A place to create and schedule your posts!

**What you can do:**

1. **Generate Content** - Let AI create posts for you!

2. **Schedule Posts** - Plan when to publish

   - Assign to an account

3. **Manage Queue** - See what's waiting
   - View pending content
   - Reorder priority
   - Cancel if needed

**Buttons:**
- ➕ **Generate** - Create new AI content
- 📅 **Schedule** - Plan when to post
- 👁️ **Preview** - See what the post will look like

**What you'll see:**
A list of your internet connections (proxies). This is technical but important!

**Why do we need proxies?**
Imagine you're hiding behind different masks to post - Instagram won't know it's the same person! Proxies help you manage multiple accounts safely.

**The table shows:**
| Column  | What It Means              |
|-------- |--------------------------  |
| Host    | The proxy server address   |
| Port    | The "door number" to enter |
| Country | Where the proxy is located |
| Status  | Is it working?             |
| Speed   | How fast it responds       |

**Buttons:**
- ➕ **Add Proxy** - Add a new proxy
- 🔄 **Test** - Check if proxy works
- 🗑️ **Delete** - Remove proxy

### 📊 PAGE 7: ANALYTICS (ANALYTQUES)

**What you'll see:**
Lots of graphs and numbers about your performance!

**Charts you'll find:**
- 📈 **Engagement over time** - Are you getting more interactions?
- 👥 **Audience growth** - How many followers?
- ❤️ **Likes & Comments** - How are people reacting?

**What the numbers mean:**

### ✨ PAGE 8: OPTIMIZATION LAB (LABORATOIRE D'OPTIMISATION)

**What you'll see:**
An AI-powered playground to make your content better!

**Features:**

1. **Caption Sandbox** - Test your captions before posting!
   - Paste your caption
   - Add hashtags
   - Click "Analyze Engagement"
   - Get a score (A, B, C, or D) 📝

2. **Optimal Times** - Find the best time to post!
   - Shows peak engagement windows
   - Based on your account's data

3. **Growth Velocity** - Get posting frequency advice!
   - Suggests how many posts per day
   - Explains why (based on your performance)

**The selector:**
At the top right, you can pick which account to analyze!

### 🔬 PAGE 9: A/B TESTING (TESTS A/B)

**What you'll see:**
A way to test different versions of your content!

**What is A/B testing?**
Imagine you have two headlines:
- A: "Buy now!" 
- B: "Don't miss out!"

You post both and see which one gets more likes! That's A/B testing!

**What you can do:**
- Create new tests
- See which variant is winning
- Track performance over time

**The table shows:**
| Column |           What It Means        |
|--------|--------------------------------|
| Name   | Test name                      |
| Status | Running or finished            |
| Winner | Which version is better        |
| Lift   | How much better is the winner? |

---

### 🚀 PAGE 10: CAMPAIGNS (CAMPAGNES)

**What you'll see:**
A table of **content strategies** — reusable plans that group a niche, topic, and Instagram account(s).

**What is a campaign?**
A saved strategy such as: *"Fitness niche, topic ‘morning routine’, accounts @brand_a and @brand_b."*  
Creating a campaign **does not** start generation automatically.

**What you can do:**
- 📋 **Create campaign** — Save name, strategy type, niche, topic, and accounts
- ▶️ **Generate** (row menu) — Launch reel generation jobs in Generation Studio for each linked account
- ⏸️ **Pause / Resume** — Stop or restart background rules (growth strategies only)
- 🗑️ **Delete** — Remove the campaign record (existing jobs keep running)
- 🎬 **Open studio** — Jump to the latest generation job for that campaign

**Strategy types:**
- **Content** — Manual generation only (you click Generate)
- **Growth** — May adjust posting frequency automatically in the background
- **Engagement** — Manual for now (use the Engagement page for DMs and comments)

**Campaign statuses:**
- ▶️ **Active** — Available for generation and (for growth) background rules
- ⏸️ **Paused** — Generation and automation paused
- ✅ **Completed** — Finished (when supported in a future update)
